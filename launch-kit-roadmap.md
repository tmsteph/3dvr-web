# 3DVR Launch Kit

## Core Rule

Sell first. Build second. Keep it simple.

## Positioning

3DVR helps people build their digital presence, income, and community instead of replacing real life with artificial experiences.

## Offer

- `Setup`: one-page website delivered fast
- `Support`: ongoing edits, hosting help, and light technical support
- `Upsell`: forms, automation, CRM, and custom build work

## Immediate Goal

Get the first 1 to 5 paying customers for a simple website offer before expanding platform scope.

## Offer Ladder

- `$99` setup for a launch site
- `$20/month` for support and lightweight updates
- `$249+` for custom pages, forms, or automation

## This Week

1. Tighten the homepage promise around simple websites and support.
2. Add or refine an intake flow.
3. Keep 3 templates ready for fast delivery.
4. Close 1 paying customer.
5. Deliver quickly and collect a testimonial.

## Build Order

1. Sales page
2. Intake form
3. Template system
4. Delivery workflow
5. Testimonials and examples

## Experimentation Track

Keep experiments small, measurable, and tied to business outcomes.

1. Test homepage headline and supporting offer copy.
2. Test CTA wording on the main homepage and subscribe page.
3. Test one pricing presentation at a time, not the whole business model at once.
4. Keep one control and one variant until traffic justifies more complexity.

## Testing Stack

- `Vercel`: host preview deployments and route experiment variants safely
- `GitHub`: keep each experiment on a branch with a PR and clear notes
- `Playwright`: catch UI regressions and snapshot important flows before rollout
- `Analytics / experiment provider`: measure conversion, clicks, and sign-up behavior

## Next Step

Start with one homepage A/B test:

- Control: practical 3dvr positioning
- Variant: soul-trip framing with grounded practical subcopy
- Metric: clicks to `Subscribe` and clicks to `Launch Your Site`

## Do Not Do Yet

- large platform rebuilds
- social network features
- deep AI automation
- hardware experiments
- complex billing expansions before clear demand

## Decision Filter

If a task does not help get or serve a paying customer now, defer it.
