FAQ

When should a team choose Embedded instead of Builder?

Choose Embedded when the workflow is shared, the coordination problem is team-wide, and you want a monthly execution lane instead of just solo-operator support.

What if the team is still small?

Small is fine. The question is not headcount alone. The question is whether intake, scheduling, and follow-up already need a real shared loop.
