Our Vision

Shared Stewardship

Technology stays rooted in the people who contribute to it and the communities who depend on it.

  • Employee and customer owners shaping strategy together.
  • Transparent governance that keeps value circulating locally.

Regenerative Craft

We design systems that protect people, their data, and the environments they call home.

  • Repairable hardware paired with healthy, joyful experiences.
  • Sustainable choices embedded into every release.

Pathways for Makers

Our services help members thrive today while preparing for the next wave of innovation.

  • Websites and support that lift up nomads, creators, and explorers.
  • Training pathways that equip and employ the world in member-led tech work.

This is only the starting point. We'll keep investing in open, community-owned systems that balance innovation, accessibility, and care for the Earth.
