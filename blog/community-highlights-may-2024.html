Community Highlights: May 2024

May 28, 2024 • Community Team • Community Spotlight

Every month we highlight the most inspiring experiences built with 3DVR. May brought ambitious storytelling, technical mastery, and new collaborations across the globe.

The three projects below exemplify how creators are using our toolset and portal integrations. Dive in, get inspired, and add your own showcase by submitting through the creator portal.

Aurora Sanctum

Studio Orison crafted Aurora Sanctum, a meditative exploration designed for remote wellness retreats. The team used volumetric lighting presets and synchronized audio cues available in the portal asset marketplace.

Why it stands out: Layered lighting sequences tied to the user's heart rate monitor create a personalized pace through the environment.

Grid Racers Live

Grid Labs turned competitive racing into a hybrid event. They leveraged the new spectator camera controls to broadcast first-person feeds directly to streaming platforms.

Why it stands out: Portal-managed match lobbies and automated tournament brackets kept hundreds of racers synced across time zones.

Story Mines

An indie collective from Montréal launched Story Mines, a collaborative narrative puzzle. Episodes drop weekly, and players' choices are aggregated through the portal analytics dashboard.

Why it stands out: Real-time branching stories powered by creator-authored logic blocks demonstrate the flexibility of our scripting engine.

Congratulations to all of the creators featured this month. We are already reviewing June submissions — share your builds with the community team to be considered for the next spotlight.
