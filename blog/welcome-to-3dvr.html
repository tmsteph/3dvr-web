Welcome to the 3DVR Blog

May 20, 2024 • 3DVR Team • Platform Updates

Today we are opening the doors to the official 3DVR blog — a dedicated home for release notes, immersive design guidance, and stories from creators who are shaping the next era of interactive worlds.

Whether you are exploring 3DVR for the first time or already building inside the platform, this space gives you a single destination to follow what is shipping, what is next, and how to get involved.

What to expect

Every post will focus on a specific theme and include actionable insights. Expect the following recurring series:

  • Product briefs: Deep dives on new features, performance improvements, and tooling updates.
  • Creator spotlights: Highlights from the 3DVR community, including the workflows and assets that power their worlds.
  • Guides and best practices: Tutorials, optimization checklists, and marketing tips to help you launch immersive experiences faster.

Connected to portal.3dvr.tech

The blog is tightly integrated with portal.3dvr.tech, so you can sign in once and participate everywhere. When you authenticate through the portal, the blog recognizes your session to enable commenting, post creation, and private preview links.

If you are part of the creator program, you will also see shortcuts to the blog editor directly inside the portal dashboard. These links are secured with your existing account permissions, ensuring editorial workflows stay streamlined.

"Our mission is to make immersive creation accessible. A transparent, community-first blog is a key step toward that mission." — The 3DVR Product Team

How to share your story

Use the Create a post link in the header to jump into the writer experience. Drafts sync to your portal workspace, meaning teammates can collaborate before anything goes live. We also publish guest contributions — send a note through the creator support channel if you have a project you want to highlight.

If you discover an issue or want to suggest topics we should cover, drop a comment below. Your feedback informs our roadmap and helps fellow builders.

We cannot wait to hear what you are building. Subscribe to the community newsletter, join the Discord, and keep an eye on this blog for the latest news. Welcome aboard!
