# Warm Lead To Member Journey (3DVR)

## Goal
Turn people from daily personal and professional conversations into active 3DVR members.

## Principle
Start from warm leads first: people who already know, trust, or have context on Thomas and 3DVR.

## Journey Stages
1. Awareness
2. Interest Discovery
3. Vision Alignment
4. Invitation
5. Follow-Up
6. Join

## Stage 1: Awareness
Use a short mission intro in normal conversation.

Script:
"I am building 3DVR. Our mission is to help people create, connect, and grow through immersive digital spaces."

## Stage 2: Interest Discovery
Ask short prompts to understand what matters to them now.

Prompts:
- "What are you most focused on right now?"
- "What are you trying to build this year?"
- "If this year goes really well, what changes for you?"

## Stage 3: Vision Alignment
Translate their goal into one concrete 3DVR value.

Examples:
- More visibility -> audience and community presence
- More clients -> member network and business touchpoints
- More creative momentum -> collaborative space and accountability

## Stage 4: Invitation
Offer one clear, low-friction next step.

Options:
- Join a live session
- Book a short onboarding call
- Join as a member directly

## Stage 5: Follow-Up (Within 24 Hours)
Send one message with a direct CTA.

Template:
"Great talking today, [Name]. You said your focus is [Goal]. 3DVR can help by [Specific Value]. Here is the link: [Link]. Reply YES and I will onboard you."

## Stage 6: Join
Complete onboarding and capture source attribution in CRM.

## Quick Outreach Script
Use this sequence in text, DM, or in-person follow-up.

1. "Hey [Name], quick update: I am building 3DVR to help people create, connect, and grow in immersive digital spaces."
2. "Can I ask you two quick questions about what you are building this year?"
3. "Based on that, I think 3DVR can help you with [Specific Value]."
4. "Would you be open to joining a short live session to see if membership is a fit?"
5. "If yes, I will send the link and a simple next step."

## Daily Operating Cadence
1. Start with 5 warm leads from daily life context.
2. Run Awareness + Discovery in the same interaction.
3. Log each lead immediately in CRM.
4. Send follow-up within 24 hours.
5. Re-contact all "Interested" leads every 7 days until a clear yes/no.

## Lead Qualification Fields
- Interests
- Vision or dream outcome
- Main pain point
- Best-fit 3DVR value
- Next agreed step

## KPIs
Track weekly:
- Warm leads contacted
- Discovery conversations completed
- Invitations sent
- Sessions attended
- Members joined
- Join rate = members joined / invitations sent
