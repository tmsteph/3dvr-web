The ladder

Monthly support and one-time delivery should feed each other.

The subscription plans are for ongoing access, support, community, and collaboration. One-time payments are for concrete deliverables.

  • Free to Supporter to Creator to Builder to Partner.
  • One-time $300 microsite to $50/month Builder support.
  • Custom build deposit to Partner if ongoing technical help is needed.

Business engine

The agent’s job is narrow enough to work.

Sell and deliver 3DVR plans and microsite services. Find leads, recommend the right offer, draft the pitch, help deploy, follow up, and turn one-time work into recurring support.

1. Find leads.Look for creators, event teams, freelancers, small businesses, and people with an idea that needs a real web presence.
2. Recommend the right product.Supporter, Creator, Builder, Partner, Quick Review, Direction Sprint, Microsite, or Custom Build.
3. Write the pitch and project intake.Use their words, define the deliverable, and make the next step obvious.
4. Draft, deploy, and follow up.Ship the page, ask for feedback, get a testimonial, and suggest Builder when monthly support makes sense.

Keep the public page simple. Keep the operating model here.

The public site can point people to a simple plan or microsite offer. This internal route keeps the broader auto-business loop available for sales, delivery, follow-up, and agent work.
