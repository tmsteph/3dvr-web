System rule

The frame carries the depth.

Instead of turning the whole site into a 3D engine, the frame becomes the controlled stage. Content layers inside it at different depths, and the rest of the page stays reliable HTML.

Why this route

A public 3D site still has to work.

The goal is not to chase a browser-fragile effect. This prototype keeps the atmosphere and layered depth while still rendering as a normal website when needed. Portal-style glow panels and HUD overlays add more energy without breaking the basic web surface.

Next ideas
