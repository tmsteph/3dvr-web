Arrival deck

Set the first 10 seconds like a club entrance, not a flat landing page.

The frame makes the homepage feel spatial right away: headline in front, world behind it, and obvious paths to move deeper into the site without forcing a game-like interface.

  • Fast first read on every screen size.
  • Motion depth without browser lockout.
  • Tap targets stay clear even inside the 3D scene.

System rule

The frame carries the depth.

Instead of turning the whole site into a 3D engine, the frame becomes the controlled stage. Content layers inside it at different depths, and the rest of the page stays reliable HTML.

Why this route

Better than a browser warning.

KODE-style mood is compelling, but a public site cannot fall apart on an older phone. This prototype keeps the atmosphere while still rendering as a normal website when needed.

Next ideas

Where this could go.

Replace the scene cards with live pages, route each zone to a membership or schedule lane, and layer audio-reactive or scroll-reactive motion once the base frame is stable.
