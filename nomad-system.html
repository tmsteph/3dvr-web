The 3dvr nomad system

This is the stack we are building over time, starting with what works now and moving toward deeper hardware and infrastructure independence.

1. Work

Websites, apps, AI tools, CRM, and support for people building a business online.

2. Device

Linux-powered laptops, portable monitors, input systems, and practical mobile setups.

3. Power and shelter

Travel gear, solar-ready systems, desk concepts, camping tools, and portable comfort.

4. Future hardware

Open hardware, low-power computing, and long-term exploration into chips and fabrication.

What we're offering first

We're starting with services, setup help, and community, then growing into gear, kits, and eventually our own physical products.
